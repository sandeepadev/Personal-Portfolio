Institute Management System (Ongoing Project)

  • Tech Stack: Java, Spring Boot, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Jquery, MySQL
  • Github URL: Project Link

Description:
Designed to streamline the administrative processes of educational institutions, this Institute Management System offers a comprehensive solution for managing various aspects of the institute, from student and teacher management to attendance tracking and financial management. Built with Java, Spring Boot, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, and MySQL, this system provides a user-friendly interface and robust functionality to meet the needs of administrators, teachers, parents, and students.

Key Features
    Student Management : Easily manage student information including enrollment, demographics, academic records, and more.
    Teacher Management : Efficiently handle teacher details, assignments, schedules, and performance evaluations.
    Parent Management : Enable parents to access their child's academic progress, attendance, and communicate with teachers.
    Classes Management : Organize classes, subjects, timetables, and resources effectively for seamless academic operations.
    Hall Management : Facilitate the reservation and management of halls or rooms for various events, meetings, or classes.
    Attendance Management : Track student and teacher attendance efficiently, generate reports, and manage exceptions.
    Payments : Manage fee structures, invoices, payment processing, and financial transactions securely.
    Reports : Generate comprehensive reports on various aspects such as attendance, academic performance, financial records, and more for informed decision-making.
